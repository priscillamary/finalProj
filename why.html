Why We Care?

Most people fail to understand the issue of Electronic waste and pollution caused by technology companies and any other organization filling up landfills with tons of reusable equipment. Combining their passions, Priscilla, Anousha, Ariana, and Jasmin came together to spread awareness about problems they care about. E-wAste affects Wildlife, our lives, and the Earth. using this websites, they wanted to expand the recycling debate to focus on a lesser-known issue within this topic. Focusing on specific effects of e-waste and how to reduce its presence,they are happy to eliminate ignorance when it comes to this topic.
