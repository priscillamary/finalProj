What's the Problem involving Animals?

- Electronics are made partly out of hazards like lead (which make up older TVs), mercury (which make up flatscreen TVs/computers), and nickel.
- Landfills build up with toxic e-waste.
- Toxic metals in e-waste can leach into our supply of resources and threatening their integrity.
- Animals are in any environment - even cities, and when cities fill up with e-waste, these animals get in the mix of yet another man-made issue.

The Vicious Cycle

- Landfills near bodies of water mix with the hazards.
- Result: contaminated water and soil.
- The food chain (all the way up to humans) is ridden with toxins.
